# ComplyFlow Video Tutorial Script

**Goal:** Produce a 10-minute walkthrough video demonstrating ComplyFlow's key features for the CodeCanyon listing and marketing website.

---

## Production Notes

- **Length:** 10 minutes (±30 seconds)
- **Resolution:** 1920 × 1080, 60 FPS preferred
- **Audio:** External microphone, record voice-over separately for clarity
- **Software:** OBS Studio (recommended) or Camtasia
- **Environment:** Fresh WordPress install with demo data loaded

---

## Outline & Timing

| Time | Scene | Talking Points |
|------|-------|----------------|
| 0:00 – 0:45 | Intro & Branding | Presenter intro, plugin value proposition, compliance challenges |
| 0:45 – 2:00 | Dashboard Tour | Compliance score, recent activity, quick actions, module cards |
| 2:00 – 3:15 | Accessibility Scanner | Launch scan, view results, highlight remediation guidance, scheduled scans |
| 3:15 – 4:30 | Consent Manager | Banner customization, geo rules, consent log filtering |
| 4:30 – 5:30 | DSR Portal | Frontend form walkthrough, admin workflow, export options |
| 5:30 – 6:30 | Cookie Inventory | Detection, categorization, link to consent banner |
| 6:30 – 7:30 | Legal Generator | Questionnaire, generated policies, snippets library |
| 7:30 – 8:30 | Analytics & Reports | Compliance trends, audit trail, PDF/CSV exports |
| 8:30 – 9:30 | Integrations | WooCommerce export sample, page builder compatibility |
| 9:30 – 10:00 | Closing & CTA | Recap benefits, invite to try demo and purchase |

---

## Detailed Script

### Scene 1 — Introduction

- “Welcome to ComplyFlow, the all-in-one compliance and accessibility suite for WordPress.”
- Mention supported regulations (WCAG 2.2, GDPR, CCPA, LGPD).
- Highlight target audience (agencies, site owners, enterprise).

### Scene 2 — Dashboard Overview

- Show compliance score badge (aim for 78/100).
- Explain quick actions and recent activity feed.
- Emphasize modular architecture and at-a-glance status.

### Scene 3 — Accessibility Scanner

- Start a scan on the sample homepage.
- Narrate severity levels and remediation steps.
- Demonstrate PDF export and scheduling options.

### Scene 4 — Consent Manager

- Display banner customization panel.
- Switch to frontend preview to show EU consent banner.
- Review consent logs, filtering by geo-region and category.

### Scene 5 — DSR Portal

- Visit public portal page, submit a sample request.
- Switch to admin to show request workflow and exports.

### Scene 6 — Cookie Inventory

- Display detected cookies categorized by purpose.
- Show how categories sync with consent preferences.

### Scene 7 — Legal Documents

- Walk through the questionnaire.
- Preview generated privacy policy and cookie policy.

### Scene 8 — Analytics & Integrations

- Highlight compliance trend charts and audit trail entries.
- Mention WooCommerce data exports and popular page builder support.

### Scene 9 — Closing Call-to-Action

- “Ready to make compliance simple? Grab ComplyFlow on CodeCanyon or test drive the live demo at demo.complyflow.com.”
- Mention included documentation and support.

---

## B-Roll & Overlays

- Overlay highlight boxes around UI elements.
- Include short text callouts: “Automated Scans,” “Geo Targeted Consent,” etc.
- Consider inserting customer testimonial quotes if available.

---

## Post-Production Checklist

- Trim dead air and cursor movement pauses.
- Normalize audio levels and remove background noise.
- Add intro/outro slide with branding and website URL.
- Export MP4 (H.264), 1080p, ≤ 500 MB for CodeCanyon.

---

Store the final video in `docs/media/video/` and upload to hosting platform (YouTube/Vimeo) for embedding and support references.
