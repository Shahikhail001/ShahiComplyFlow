# Configuring Consent Banner Appearance

## Overview
Customize the look and behavior of your consent banner to match your site's branding and compliance needs.

## Steps
1. Go to **WordPress Admin → ComplyFlow → Settings → Consent Manager**.
2. Adjust banner position (top, bottom, modal), colors, and text.
3. Choose which consent categories to display (Necessary, Analytics, Marketing, etc.).
4. Enable or disable geo-targeting for region-specific banners.
5. Use the preview feature to see changes in real time.
6. Click **Save Settings** to apply changes.

## Tips
- Use multi-language support for international audiences.
- Test the banner on different devices and browsers.
- Regularly review settings to stay compliant with new regulations.
