# CSV Export of Compliance Data

## Overview
Export compliance data, scan results, consent logs, or cookie inventory as CSV files for analysis or documentation.

## Steps
1. Go to the relevant section (Dashboard, Accessibility, Consent Logs, Cookie Inventory, etc.).
2. Click the **Export CSV** button.
3. Save the CSV file to your computer.
4. Open the file in Excel or another spreadsheet tool for review.

## Tips
- Use CSV exports for audits, reporting, or sharing with your legal team.
- Regularly export logs for backup and compliance.
- Check column headers for data context.
