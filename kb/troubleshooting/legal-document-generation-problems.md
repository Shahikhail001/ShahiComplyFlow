# Legal Document Generation Problems

## Problem
Legal documents fail to generate, save, or display correctly.

## Solution
- Complete all required steps in the questionnaire.
- Check for JavaScript or PHP errors in the browser console and debug log.
- Ensure all plugin files and assets are up to date.
- Review template and section settings for missing or invalid data.

## Tips
- Use the version history to restore previous document versions.
- Contact support with error logs and screenshots if issues persist.
