# Creating Legal Documents via Questionnaire

## Overview
Generate privacy policies, terms of service, and cookie policies using the guided questionnaire.

## Steps
1. Go to **WordPress Admin → ComplyFlow → Legal Documents**.
2. Click **Create New Policy**.
3. Complete each step of the 8-step questionnaire (company info, data types, legal basis, vendors, retention, rights, cookies, contact).
4. Review the generated policy in the editor.
5. Make any necessary edits or customizations.
6. Click **Save** to store the policy.
7. Use the provided shortcode to publish the policy on your site.

## Tips
- The questionnaire auto-detects plugins and integrations for relevant clauses.
- Use the preview to check formatting before publishing.
- Regularly update your policies as your business or regulations change.
