# Managing Cookie Inventory

## Overview
Keep your cookie inventory up to date for compliance and transparency.

## Steps
1. Go to **WordPress Admin → ComplyFlow → Cookie Inventory**.
2. Click **Scan Now** to detect all cookies and trackers.
3. Review detected cookies in the dashboard table.
4. Assign each cookie to the correct category (Necessary, Analytics, etc.).
5. Add or edit cookies manually if needed.
6. Use bulk actions to update multiple cookies at once.
7. Export the inventory as CSV for documentation or audits.

## Tips
- Regularly scan for new cookies after plugin/theme updates.
- Keep categories aligned with your cookie policy.
- Use the audit trail to track inventory changes.
