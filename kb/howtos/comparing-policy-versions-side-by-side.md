# Comparing Policy Versions Side-by-Side

## Overview
Visually compare two versions of a legal document to see what has changed.

## Steps
1. Go to **WordPress Admin → ComplyFlow → Legal Documents**.
2. Click **Edit** on the policy you want to review.
3. Click the **Version History** button.
4. Select two versions and click **Compare with Previous**.
5. Review the color-coded diff (green for additions, red for removals, yellow for modifications).

## Tips
- Use the legend to understand diff colors.
- Always review changes before publishing updates.
- Comparing versions helps with audits and legal reviews.
