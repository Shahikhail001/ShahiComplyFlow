# Setting Up Automated Compliance Tracking

## Overview
Automate compliance monitoring and reporting to stay ahead of regulatory requirements.

## Steps
1. Go to **WordPress Admin → ComplyFlow → Settings → Accessibility**.
2. Set up scheduled accessibility scans (daily, weekly, etc.).
3. Enable compliance history tracking in settings.
4. Configure email notifications for scan results and compliance events.
5. Review compliance trends and reports in the dashboard.

## Tips
- Use scheduled scans to catch issues early.
- Export compliance history for audits.
- Combine automated tracking with manual reviews for best results.
